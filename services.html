Paid Social vs. Paid Search

Let’s get some PPC jargon out of the way. The difference between paid social and paid search digital marketing helps you understand why both are important to have individually and even more important to run together. They feed each other in a synergistic way resulting in exponential success.

  • Paid Social

    Push Marketing is our paid social tactic.

    This type of marketing strategy hopes to minimize the amount of time between a customer discovering a product and buying your product.

    Here we create interest where there may or may not have already been some.

    Essentially, paid social is targeting specific audiences with ads on Facebook, IG, and other social media platforms.

    Interruption-based marketing.

  • Paid Search

    Pull Marketing fits right under paid search.

    This method works best when you’re wanting.

    Here we capture interests that already exist.

    In essence, paid search is getting to the top of people’s search engine inquiries.

    Intent-based marketing.
